Lasagna Recipe

Description

Classic Meat Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food—rich, hearty, and layered with love. Tender sheets of pasta are stacked between a savory, slow-simmered meat sauce made with ground beef and garlic-scented marinara. A creamy ricotta cheese mixture, seasoned with herbs and Parmesan, adds a luscious contrast to the robust tomato sauce. Melted mozzarella crowns each layer, bubbling to golden perfection in the oven. After a brief rest, every slice holds together beautifully, delivering a balanced bite of cheesy, meaty, and saucy goodness. It's a timeless dish perfect for Sunday dinners, family gatherings, or anytime you crave a cozy, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce or 3 cups homemade tomato sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried basil or Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain fat. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Assemble:
    • Spread ½ cup sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    • Layer 3 noodles, ⅓ of ricotta mix, ⅓ of sauce, and ⅓ of mozzarella. Repeat for 3 layers.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 more minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let sit 10 minutes before serving.
