Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken Recipe

Description

This creamy garlic Parmesan chicken is rich, comforting, and packed with flavor. Tender pan-seared chicken breasts are simmered in a velvety garlic Parmesan sauce, perfect for serving over pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken 5–7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and scrape up any browned bits. Add cream and Parmesan. Stir until smooth and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Return chicken to the pan and spoon sauce over it. Simmer 3–5 minutes until heated through.
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
