Vegetarian Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe

Description

This creamy garlic Parmesan chicken is rich, comforting, and packed with flavor. Tender pan-seared chicken breasts are simmered in a velvety garlic Parmesan sauce, perfect for serving over pasta, rice, or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color), tops cut off and seeds removed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (or canned)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or your choice)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add beans, corn, tomatoes, rice, and spices. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring often. Remove from heat and stir in half the cheese.
  4. Stuff each bell pepper with the mixture and place in a baking dish. Top with remaining cheese.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 10–15 minutes until peppers are tender and cheese is melted.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs or a dollop of sour cream if desired.
